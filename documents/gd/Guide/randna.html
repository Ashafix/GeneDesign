GeneDesign Modules: Random DNA Sequence Generation

GeneDesign's Random DNA Sequence Generator module allows you to create a DNA sequence of order and defined composition.

You can define length (bp), AT content, and a number of sequences to generate. You can also choose to disallow stop codons in the first frame of translation (random ORFs).

The next screen will give you a copy-able list of the sequences generated.
