GeneDesign Modules: Vector Filter

GeneDesign's Vector Filter module allows you to search and sort the vectors known to the program by a variety of criteria.

You can select vectors by size (kb), the number of absent sites, and the presence or absence of specific restriction enzymes.

The next screen will give you a copy-able sorted list of vectors that match your criteria, as well as an annotated list for your further edification.
