Error Reporting
 
GeneDesign 3.0

Thank you for using GeneDesign's error reporting form. The more information you provide, the better I will be able to address the problem. You do not need to send sensitive sequence information.

I will need information about your computer (operating system), your browser (program name and version), the protein sequence you used, the vector sequence you used (if applicable), enzyme information (which were supposed to be absent, which were supposed to be removed, etc) and a note describing the problem and where it occured. All of this will help me to recreate and isolate the error. If you leave your e-mail I will get back to you.

To find out which operating system you have:

Macintosh OS: While in the Finder, go to the top left hand corner of the screen and select "About this Mac" from the apple menu. The version number should appear.

Windows: Right click on "My Computer" and select "Properties". The version should appear.
